About Us

This is in regards to introduce ourselves as a leading supplier of all kind of indian handicrafts our product including Table Ware, Furniture, Lightning products,Kitchen ware,Garden Accessories,Home Decor etc.


Modworld International is India’s one of the fastest growing handicrafts export company, offering customers every choice in terms of materials like; wood, glass, metal and stone with multiple finishes. At Modworld, we provide sophisticated, one-stop-shop solutions to meet our customers’ specific needs and helps to create a platter of offerings where they have more choice, more options to choose from. Today we have evolved to a point, where-in we are catering from exclusive designers to mass retailers, equally effectively.


Our global team of designers and trend-experts work daily to ensure that our products shall be tuned to the latest trends and with reliable global compliance. We have well developed infrastructure & support systems, adding backward integration processes, commissioning a number of machines and improving the quality of man force through skill-development programs. We are one of the most important innovators in the handicraft’s exports market. We are committed to achieving the highest standards when meeting the needs of our customers through competitive pricing, quality, and reliable delivery. By leveraging our global network, market knowledge, and advanced technology, we have the capacity and flexibility to rapidly respond to evolving trends in consumer and production markets.


Our business is built upon our people, our time-honoured values, our approach to sustainability through the entire supply chain, and our community engagement. Our global network, our financial strength, and our leadership are the platform for our continued success and future growth.


Speciality

We are specialised in fusing all the materials together to create beautiful products. Our nature is specialised and customised product development. We translate imagination and concepts into reality.


Services

We want you to know that we do not only sell or manufacture products. We sell possibilities and manufacture opportunities. We are creating collections and themes, based on intensive market research and focus on certain core shapes and colours so that different markets and tastes can be served, without losing the focus.


We show concepts, not only individual products, themes, collections and mixing categories and sub-categories, creating an interesting "lifestyle"-look.

CURRENTLY WORKING

Modworld International is India’s one of the fastest growing company in handicraft products and we are working in many countries like – Europe, America & Australia.


OUR VISION

Be the world’s premier handicrafts manufacturing and exports organization by achieving extraordinary results for our customers, building satisfying careers for our people, and earning a fair return on the value we deliver.
